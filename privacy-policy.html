Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

Tuna ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Profile Information: Name, age, weight, height, and dietary preferences (allergies, food intolerances)
  • Scanned Images: Photos of food labels you choose to scan

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app (scans performed, features used)
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version

Information from Third Parties

  • Subscription Data: If you subscribe to Tuna Pro, we receive transaction confirmations from Apple (we do not see your payment details)

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Analyze food labels for allergens based on your profile
  • Personalize your experience with relevant health insights
  • Maintain and improve our services
  • Process subscription purchases

Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Your profile and scan history are stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Services: We use Firebase for app configuration and Google Gemini AI for image analysis
  • No Account Required: We do not require account creation; your data stays on your device

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Firebase: App configuration and analytics
  • Google Gemini AI: Food label analysis
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Apple App Store: In-app purchase processing

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete Your Data: Clear all scan history and profile data from Settings
  • Control Permissions: Manage camera and photo library access in device settings

Children's Privacy

Tuna is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Medical Disclaimer

Tuna provides informational content only and is not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always consult a healthcare provider for allergy-related medical decisions.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app.
